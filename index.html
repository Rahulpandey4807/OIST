Intermediate

Our Colleges

Oriental Institute Of Science And Technology

OIST is a leading technical campus within the Oriental Group of Institutions. It offers a diverse array of engineering and technology programs, emphasizing practical skills and innovation, and is known for its state-of-the-art laboratories and research opportunities.

Oriental College of Technology

OCT is another esteemed campus specializing in engineering and management disciplines. It provides a comprehensive education with a focus on entrepreneurship and industry-relevant skills, preparing students for successful careers in the corporate world.

Oriental College Of Pharmacy

OCP is dedicated to pharmaceutical education and research. With modern laboratories and experienced faculty, it offers programs that equip students with the knowledge and expertise needed to excel in the pharmaceutical and healthcare sectors.

Oriental College of Management

OCM is the hub for business and management studies within the Oriental Group. It offers a wide range of management programs, fostering leadership and managerial skills while emphasizing practical exposure through internships and industry collaborations.

Our Campus
